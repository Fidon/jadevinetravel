About This Manual

This manual covers the Jadevine Travel & Tours platform — a booking system for hotels, safari packages, and car rentals in Zanzibar and Tanzania. The system has three user types, each with distinct access levels.

Portal vs Public Site: The staff portal is accessed at /portal/. Customers access the main website and their dashboard at /accounts/dashboard/. These are completely separate login systems — a customer account cannot access the portal.

Super Admin

Logging In to the Portal

The staff portal uses a separate login from the public website. You log in with a username and password, not an email address.

How to Log In

Navigate to /portal/login/ in your browser.

  1. Enter your username in the Username field. Usernames are case-insensitive — "Admin", "admin", and "ADMIN" all work the same.

  2. Enter your password and click Sign In.

  3. You will be taken to the Portal Dashboard.

Portal access only: Only accounts with staff status can log in here. If you see an "Access denied" message, contact the developer to verify your account has been set up correctly.

Session & Security

  • Your session stays active for 8 hours of inactivity.
  • Always click Sign Out when finished, especially on shared computers.
  • If changing your password, your session stays active — you will not be logged out.
Super Admin

The Dashboard

The dashboard gives you a real-time overview of the platform — bookings, revenue, pending items, and recent activity.

What You See

Pending Approvals (Top Priority)

The most important section. Shown at the very top so you never miss a listing waiting for review.

  • Hotels pending — count of hotel listings submitted by mini-admins awaiting your review.
  • Cars pending — count of car rental listings awaiting review.
  • Quick links to the pending queues for Hotels and Cars.
  • If all clear, a green "All listings reviewed" badge appears.

Booking Statistics

  • Today's confirmed bookings and revenue.
  • This week's and this month's totals.
  • Breakdown by status: Confirmed, Pending, Cancelled, Completed.

Recent Bookings Table

The last 10 bookings across all services. Click any row to view full booking details.

Unread Contact Messages

A count of new contact form submissions with a quick link to the messages section.

Pending Reviews

Customer reviews awaiting moderation approval.

Live Count Updates

The pending counts badge in the sidebar and dashboard updates automatically every 60 seconds — no need to refresh the page.

Super Admin

Hotels Management

Create, edit, and manage all hotel listings. You have full access to all hotels — including those created by mini-admins.

The Hotel List

Navigate to /portal/hotels/

The table shows all hotels with their name, location, star rating, owner (created by), approval status, and visibility. Filter by approval status, location, or owner using the dropdowns above the table.

Use the Pending filter to quickly see all listings awaiting your review, or click the pending count badge in the sidebar to jump straight to the pending queue.

Creating a Hotel

  1. Click Add Hotel on the Hotels page.

  2. Fill in the hotel details: Name, Location, Description (English, French, Russian), Star Rating, Price per Night, address, and TripAdvisor URL if available.

  3. Add room types using the room type section — each room type has its own name, price, max guests, amenities, and refund/POA policy.

  4. Upload photos using the photo upload area. You can drag to reorder and set a cover photo.

  5. Click Save Hotel. Because you are Super Admin, the listing goes live immediately — no approval step required.

Super Admin listings skip the approval queue. They are set to Approved and Live on creation automatically. Only mini-admin listings require your review.

Editing a Hotel

  1. Find the hotel in the list and click Edit (pencil icon).

  2. Update any details across the tabs: General Info, Room Types, Photos.

  3. Click Save Changes. Your edits go live immediately — no approval reset occurs when a Super Admin edits a listing.

Editing a mini-admin's approved listing: When you edit an approved mini-admin listing, the approval status is not reset — your changes are trusted and take effect immediately.

Photo Management

  • Click Upload Photos on the hotel detail page.
  • Photos upload one at a time in a queue — wait for each to complete before the next starts.
  • Drag photos in the grid to reorder them.
  • Click the star icon on a photo to set it as the cover (shown on listing cards).
  • Click the trash icon to delete a photo — a confirmation dialog appears first.

Toggling Visibility

On any approved hotel, use the Active/Hidden toggle to temporarily hide it from the public site without changing its approval status. Useful for hotels that are temporarily closed or under renovation.

Super Admin

Tours & Safaris Management

Tour packages are managed exclusively by Super Admin. There is no mini-admin access to tours and no approval workflow — all tour changes go live immediately.

Creating a Tour Package

  1. Navigate to /portal/tours/ and click Add Tour.

  2. Fill in the package name (English, French, Russian), tour type (Safari, Beach, Cultural, Climbing, or Combined), and cover image.

  3. Enter duration, max group size, and price per person in USD.

  4. Add highlights, inclusions, and exclusions — one item per line in the text area. They are saved as a list automatically.

  5. Add the day-by-day itinerary using the Itinerary Days section. Click Add Day, enter the day number, title, and description. Repeat for each day.

  6. Upload tour photos. Note: unlike hotels, tour photos have no cover — the tour cover is the Cover Image field set at the top of the form.

  7. Toggle Featured if this tour should appear in the homepage Featured Packages section.

  8. Click Save Tour.

Managing Itinerary Days

  • Days are ordered automatically by day number.
  • Click the edit icon on a day row to open the edit modal and update its title or description.
  • Click the trash icon to delete a day — a confirmation appears first.
  • Each day number must be unique for a package — you cannot have two Day 3s.
Super Admin

The Approvals Queue

When mini-admins create or resubmit listings, they land here for your review before going live on the public site.

How the Workflow Works

1
Mini-admin creates or edits a listing

The listing is saved with status Pending. It is not visible on the public site.

2
You are notified

The pending count badge in the sidebar updates. An email notification is sent to you automatically.

3A
You Approve

Status becomes Approved and the listing goes Live on the public site. The mini-admin receives an email notification.

3B
You Reject

Status becomes Rejected. You must provide a rejection reason. The mini-admin receives an email with your reason and can edit and resubmit.

Approving a Listing

  1. Go to /portal/hotels/pending/ or click the pending count badge in the dashboard.

  2. Click the listing name to open its full detail page — review all the content and photos before deciding.

  3. Click the Approve button. The listing goes live immediately. The mini-admin is notified by email.

Rejecting a Listing

  1. From the pending queue or the listing detail page, click Reject.

  2. A modal appears requiring a rejection reason — this is mandatory and will be shown to the mini-admin.

  3. Write a clear, specific reason (e.g. "Photos are too low quality — please upload higher resolution images") and click Confirm Rejection.

The rejection reason is shown directly to the mini-admin in their portal. Write it professionally — it is the guidance they need to fix the listing and resubmit.

Editing Before Approving

If a listing needs only minor corrections (e.g. a typo in the name), you can edit it directly from the detail page before approving. This saves the mini-admin a full resubmit cycle. Your edits as Super Admin do not reset approval state.

Super Admin

Bookings Management

View, filter, and manage all bookings across hotels, tours, and car rentals.

The Bookings Table

Navigate to /portal/bookings/

Filter bookings by service type (Hotel / Tour / Car), status, payment status, and date range. The table uses DataTables — you can also export to Excel, PDF, or Print using the buttons above the table.

Booking Statuses Explained

StatusMeaning
Pending ConfirmationBooking created but not yet confirmed. For tours, Jadevine must confirm the date. For Pay Now bookings, payment may be pending.
ConfirmedBooking is confirmed. Payment received (Pay Now) or slot held (Pay on Arrival).
Cancellation RequestedCustomer has requested cancellation and a refund is due. Requires manual processing.
CancelledBooking is cancelled. No action needed.
CompletedService delivered. Mark this manually after the customer's trip.
No ShowPay on Arrival customer did not arrive.

Actions You Can Take

Confirming a Tour Date

Tour bookings always start as Pending Confirmation because the customer selects a preferred date that Jadevine must confirm. Open the booking and click Confirm Date to move it to Confirmed.

Marking Pay on Arrival as Paid

When a Pay on Arrival customer pays in person, open the booking and click Mark as Paid to update the payment status to Paid.

Marking as Completed or No Show

After a service is delivered, open the booking and change the status to Completed. For no-shows, change to No Show.

Processing Cancellation Exceptions

If a customer requests a cancellation exception (e.g. emergency), open the booking and use the cancellation override option. You can apply any refund amount and add a written reason.

Super Admin

Review Moderation

All customer reviews require moderation before appearing on the public site. You can approve or reject any review across all listings.

How Reviews Work

  • Customers can only submit a review for a booking with status Completed.
  • Each booking allows exactly one review — duplicates are blocked at database level.
  • All reviews start as Pending until you act on them.
  • A listing's rating badge only appears when it has 1 or more approved reviews.

Approving a Review

  1. Navigate to /portal/reviews/

  2. Find the review in the Pending tab and click Approve. The review appears on the public listing immediately.

Rejecting a Review

  1. Click Reject on the review row.

  2. Enter a rejection reason — this is required and will be shown to the customer on their booking detail page.

  3. Click Confirm Rejection.

Rejection reasons are visible to the customer. Use professional, clear language explaining why the review was not approved (e.g. "Review contains inappropriate language").

Super Admin

Gallery Management

Manage the public gallery page — photos, videos, categories, and which items appear in the homepage Gallery Highlights section.

Categories

Gallery items are organised into categories (e.g. Safaris, Beaches, Wildlife, Hotels). You can add, edit, and delete categories using the category panel on the left of the gallery page.

  • Each category has a name in English, French, and Russian.
  • Categories with existing items cannot be deleted — remove items first.
  • The order of categories controls how they appear in the filter tabs on the public gallery.

Uploading Photos

  1. Select a category from the left panel to make it active.

  2. Click the upload area or drag and drop image files onto it.

  3. Each photo uploads one at a time. Wait for the progress indicator before uploading another.

  4. Add a caption (optional) for each photo after uploading.

Adding Videos

Paste a YouTube or Vimeo embed URL into the video URL field and click Add Video. The video appears in the gallery grid with a play icon.

Featured Items (Homepage Highlights)

Toggle the star icon on any gallery item to feature it. Featured items appear in the Gallery Highlights section on the homepage. Aim for 6–9 featured items — a mix of photos and videos works best.

Reordering Items

Drag items within the gallery grid to reorder them. The order is saved automatically.

Viewing Messages

Navigate to /portal/messages/

Filter by inquiry type (General, Custom Tour, Partnership, Press/Media) and by status (New, In Progress, Resolved).

Replying to a Message

  1. Click the message row to open its full detail page.

Updating Status

Use the status dropdown on the message detail page to mark a message as In Progress (you're working on it) or Resolved (done). This keeps your inbox organised.

Subscriber List

Navigate to /portal/newsletter/

The table shows each subscriber's email, date subscribed, and active status. Use the DataTables export buttons to download the full list as Excel, PDF, or CSV for use in your email marketing tools (e.g. Mailchimp).

Super Admin

Customer Accounts

View, search, and manage registered customer accounts. You cannot create customer accounts — customers self-register on the public website.

Searching for a User

Navigate to /portal/users/

Use the search box to find a customer by name or email address. Click a user row to view their full profile and complete booking history.

Deactivating / Reactivating

To prevent a customer from logging in (e.g. for abuse or fraud), click Deactivate on their profile page. Their account data and booking history are preserved. Click Reactivate to restore access.

Deactivating a user immediately invalidates their active session — they are logged out straight away.

Password Reset

If a customer cannot reset their own password, click Send Password Reset Email on their profile page. This sends a reset link to their registered email address.

Super Admin

Mini-Admin Management

Create and manage partner accounts for hotel or car rental representatives.

Creating a Mini-Admin Account

  1. Navigate to /portal/mini-admins/add/

  2. Fill in: Username (lowercase letters and numbers only, at least 2 characters), Email, First Name, and Last Name.

  3. Click Create Account. A welcome email is sent to the mini-admin automatically with their username, a link to set their password, and the portal URL.

No password is set by you. The mini-admin receives an email with a secure link to set their own password. This link expires after a limited time — if they miss it, use the Reset Password action to send a new one.

Deactivating a Mini-Admin

When a partnership ends, click Deactivate on the mini-admin's profile page. They can no longer log in. Their listings remain in the system — you can manage them directly as Super Admin.

Resetting a Mini-Admin's Password

Click Send Password Reset Email on the mini-admin's profile. A new secure link is sent to their email address.

Important: The password reset link for portal staff goes to /portal/set-password/ — not the allauth reset URL. This is intentional. Never share the allauth reset URL with portal staff.

Super Admin

Cancellation Policies

Configure the refund tiers that apply when customers cancel bookings. Changes apply to new bookings only — existing bookings are unaffected.

Default Policy Tiers

Days Before ServiceRefundApplies To
14+ days100% (full refund)Hotels, Tours, Cars
7–13 days50% refundHotels, Tours, Cars
0–6 days0% (no refund)Hotels, Tours, Cars

These are configurable — not hardcoded. Navigate to /portal/policies/ to edit, add, or deactivate tiers. Changes apply to bookings made after the change only.

Per-Listing Overrides

Individual listings (hotels at room-type level, cars and tours at listing level) can be set as Non-refundable. When a booking is made for a non-refundable listing, the global policy tiers are bypassed — no refund is issued regardless of cancellation timing. This is set per listing, not here.

Mini-Admin

Mini-Admin Overview

As a Mini-Admin, you are a partner representative (hotel manager or car rental operator) with access to manage your own listings and view bookings for those listings.

What You Can Do

  • Create hotel or car rental listings from scratch.
  • Upload and manage photos for your listings.
  • Edit your listings (subject to re-approval if the listing is already live).
  • View bookings made for your listings (read-only).
  • Moderate customer reviews for your own hotel and car listings.
  • Change your own password.

What You Cannot Do

  • View or edit other partners' listings.
  • Approve or reject listings (including your own).
  • Modify bookings — contact Super Admin for any booking changes.
  • Access gallery, newsletter, contact messages, or user management.
  • Create or manage tour packages — these are Jadevine's own content.
Mini-Admin

Logging In

You use the same portal login page as the Super Admin. Your access is limited to your own listings and data once inside.

First-Time Login

  1. Check your email for a welcome message from Jadevine. It contains your username and a link to set your password.

  2. Click the link in the email — it takes you to a Set Password page at /portal/set-password/.

  3. Enter and confirm your new password, then click Save Password.

  4. You are redirected to /portal/login/ — log in with your username and new password.

The password setup link expires. If you did not receive the email or the link has expired, contact Super Admin to send a new reset link.

Subsequent Logins

After your first login, simply go to /portal/login/ and enter your username and password.

Mini-Admin

Creating a Listing

You can create hotel listings and car rental listings. Tours are managed by Jadevine directly.

Creating a Hotel Listing

  1. In the sidebar, click Hotels → Add Hotel.

  2. Fill in the hotel details: Name, Location (Zanzibar or Dar es Salaam), star rating, price per night, and address.

Your listing is not live yet. It is saved with status Pending and sent to the Super Admin for review. You will receive an email when it is approved or rejected.

Creating a Car Rental Listing

In the sidebar, click Cars → Add Car. Fill in the vehicle details (type, make, model, year, capacity, fuel type, transmission, price per day, pickup locations) and save. Same approval process applies.

Editing a Listing

You can edit any of your own listings. The effect depends on the listing's current approval status.

Current StatusWhat Happens When You Save

Mini-Admin

Resubmitting a Rejected Listing

If your listing is rejected, you can fix the issues and resubmit for another review.

How to Resubmit

  1. Open the rejected listing. The rejection reason is shown prominently at the top of the page — read it carefully.

  2. Click Edit and make the necessary changes as described in the rejection reason.

  3. Save your changes.

  4. Click the Resubmit for Approval button that appears after saving. The status changes back to Pending and the Super Admin is notified.

You can resubmit as many times as needed. There is no limit on resubmissions. Address all points in the rejection reason before resubmitting to minimise back-and-forth.

Customer

Customer Guide Overview

This section covers everything you need to book hotels, safari tours, and car rentals with Jadevine Travel & Tours — from creating an account to managing your bookings.

About the Platform

Jadevine Travel & Tours is your online booking platform for Zanzibar and Tanzania. You can browse and book:

  • Hotels — accommodations in Zanzibar and Dar es Salaam.
  • Safari & Tour Packages — guided experiences including Serengeti safaris, beach tours, and cultural packages.
  • Car Rentals — vehicles with or without a bilingual driver across Tanzania.

You must create a free account and verify your email address to complete a booking.

Customer

Creating an Account

Registration is free and takes less than two minutes.

How to Register

  1. Click Create Account in the top navigation bar, or go to /accounts/signup/

  2. Fill in your First Name, Last Name, Email Address, and a Password.

  3. Choose your Preferred Language (English, French, or Russian) — this controls what language the site uses for you.

  4. Click Create Account.

  5. Check your email inbox for a verification link from Jadevine Travel & Tours.

  6. Click the link in the email — your account is activated and you can now sign in.

You must verify your email before you can log in and book. Check your spam or junk folder if the email doesn't appear within a few minutes. The verification link is valid for 3 days.

Didn't Receive the Email?

  1. Check your spam/junk folder — automated emails sometimes land there.

  2. Go to /accounts/login/ and attempt to log in. If your email isn't verified, a link to Resend Verification Email will appear.

  3. Click it and check your inbox again.

Customer

Signing In

How to Sign In

  1. Click Sign In in the navigation bar or go to /accounts/login/

  2. Enter your email address and password.

  3. Click Sign In. You are taken to your personal dashboard.

Forgot Your Password?

  1. On the sign in page, click Forgot password?

  2. Enter your email address and click Send Reset Link.

  3. Check your inbox for a reset email. Click the link inside.

  4. Enter and confirm your new password, then click Save.

The password reset link expires after 3 days. If it has expired, go through the Forgot Password process again.

Customer

Browsing & Filtering Listings

Find the perfect hotel, tour, or car using the search and filter tools on each listing page.

Hotels

Filter by: Destination (Zanzibar or Dar es Salaam), Check-in / Check-out dates, Number of guests, Star rating, and Max price per night. Results update automatically as you change filters — no need to press a search button.

Tours & Safaris

Filter by: Tour type (Safari, Beach, Cultural, Climbing, Combined), Max duration in days, and Max price per person.

Car Rentals

Filter by: Vehicle type (Sedan, SUV, Minibus, 4x4, Van), rental mode (With Driver or Self Drive), and max price per day.

Ratings & Reviews

Listings with 1 or more approved customer reviews display a rating badge (out of 10) on the listing card. Click a listing to read full reviews on the detail page.

Customer

Making a Booking

The booking process is the same across hotels, tours, and cars — a short multi-step flow.

Step 1 — Choose Your Service & Dates

On the listing detail page, fill in the booking panel on the right side:

  • Hotels: Select a room type, check-in date, check-out date, and number of guests.
  • Tours: Enter your preferred start date and number of participants.
  • Cars: Select rental mode (self-drive or with driver), pickup location, pickup date, and return date. If self-drive, enter your driver's licence number.

Add any special requests in the text box (optional). Click Review Booking.

Step 2 — Review Your Booking

A summary page shows your booking details and price breakdown. Check everything carefully:

  • Service name and type.
  • Dates and duration.
  • Price per unit × number of units = total.
  • Refund policy (Free cancellation or Non-refundable).

You must be signed in to proceed. If you are not signed in, you will be prompted to sign in or create an account. Your booking details are saved in your session — you will not lose them.

Select your payment method (see Payment Options section) and click Confirm Booking. Your booking is created at this point — not before.

Step 3 — Payment

If you selected Pay Now, you are directed to the secure payment page to complete the transaction. If you selected Pay on Arrival, you go directly to the confirmation page.

Customer

Payment Options

Jadevine offers two ways to pay — immediately online, or in person when you arrive in Tanzania.

Pay Now (Online Payment)

Pay immediately and securely online. Your booking is confirmed instantly.

  • Visa and Mastercard.
  • M-Pesa, Airtel Money, and other mobile money options.
  • All payments processed securely through PesaPal — no card details are stored by Jadevine.

Pay on Arrival

Reserve your slot now and pay in cash or card when you arrive in Tanzania. Your booking is held for you.

  • Available for directly-managed hotels, tours, and car rentals.
  • Not available for all listings — some require payment in advance.
  • A confirmation email is sent with your booking reference and the amount due on arrival.

If you selected Pay on Arrival and cannot make it, please cancel your booking as early as possible. Cancellation policies apply. No-shows may affect your ability to book in future.

Cancelling a Booking

You can cancel a booking from your dashboard. The refund amount depends on how far in advance you cancel.

Refund Policy

Days Before ServiceRefund
14 or more days before100% — full refund
7–13 days before50% refund
Less than 7 days beforeNo refund
Non-refundable bookingNo refund regardless of timing

The booking detail page shows you exactly how many days until your service date and the applicable refund amount before you confirm the cancellation. You can review this before proceeding.

How to Cancel

  1. Open the booking detail page from My Bookings.

  2. Click the Cancel Booking button. It only appears if the booking is still cancellable.

  3. A confirmation dialog shows the refund amount you are entitled to.

  4. Optionally enter a reason for cancellation.

  5. Tick "I understand and confirm this cancellation" and click Confirm Cancellation.

Receiving Your Refund

If a refund applies, Jadevine will process it within 2–3 business days. The refund is returned to your original payment method. You will receive an email confirming the refund amount.

Pay on Arrival bookings can be cancelled without a refund consideration (since you haven't paid yet). Simply cancel from your dashboard and your slot is released.

Leaving a Review

Share your experience to help other travellers make informed decisions.

When Can You Review?

You can submit a review for a booking once its status is Completed. The review form appears automatically on the booking detail page when this condition is met.

  • You can submit one review per booking.
  • Reviews are moderated before appearing publicly — this typically takes 1–2 business days.

How to Submit a Review

  1. Open the completed booking detail page from My Bookings.

  2. Scroll down to the Your Review section.

  3. Click a star to set your rating (1–10 scale).

  4. Optionally write a comment about your experience.

  5. Click Submit Review.

After Submitting

Your review shows as Pending on your booking detail page while it awaits moderation. Once approved, it appears on the public listing. If rejected, the reason will be shown on your booking detail page.
